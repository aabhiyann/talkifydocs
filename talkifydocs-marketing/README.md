## TalkifyDocs Blog & Social Content

This folder contains adapted versions of the TalkifyDocs case study for various platforms.

### Files Overview

| File                            | Platform               | Length      | Best For                          |
| ------------------------------- | ---------------------- | ----------- | --------------------------------- |
| `01-linkedin-article.md`        | LinkedIn Article       | ~1200 words | Long-form, professional audience  |
| `02-linkedin-short-post.md`     | LinkedIn Post          | ~400 words  | Quick engagement, feed visibility |
| `03-twitter-thread.md`          | Twitter/X              | 12–15 tweets| Tech audience, virality potential |
| `04-dev-to-medium.md`           | Dev.to/Medium/Hashnode | ~2000 words | SEO, technical depth, evergreen   |
| `05-reddit-post.md`             | Reddit                 | ~500 words  | Community discussion, feedback    |

### Posting Strategy (Suggested)

#### Week 1: LinkedIn Short Post

- **File:** `02-linkedin-short-post.md`
- **Goal:** Quick engagement, drive traffic to portfolio and live demo
- **Best time:** Tuesday or Wednesday, 9–11 AM local time
- **CTA:** Link to live demo and portfolio case study

#### Week 2–3: Twitter/X Thread

- **File:** `03-twitter-thread.md`
- **Goal:** Reach broader tech/AI community, potential virality
- **Best time:** Weekday, 10 AM–2 PM
- **CTA:** Encourage replies/RTs, link to demo and GitHub

#### Week 3–4: Dev.to/Medium

- **File:** `04-dev-to-medium.md`
- **Goal:** SEO, evergreen technical content, credibility
- **Best time:** Anytime (evergreen)
- **CTA:** Follow for more content, connect on LinkedIn, link to portfolio

#### Week 4–5: LinkedIn Article

- **File:** `01-linkedin-article.md`
- **Goal:** Thought leadership, deeper narrative for recruiters/leads
- **Best time:** Tuesday or Thursday, 9–11 AM
- **CTA:** Ask for experiences with production AI apps / RAG systems

#### Week 5–6: Reddit

- **File:** `05-reddit-post.md`
- **Goal:** Community feedback, technical discussion, traffic
- **Best subreddits:** r/webdev, r/reactjs, r/MachineLearning, r/cscareerquestions
- **Best time:** Weekday mornings

### Engagement Tips

#### LinkedIn

- Post during peak hours mid-week.
- Reply to comments within the first hour.
- Use 3–5 focused hashtags.
- Consider pinning the best-performing post to your profile.

#### Twitter/X

- Lead with a strong hook + visual.
- Engage quickly with comments and quote tweets.
- Re-surface the thread after 24–48 hours with a “ICYMI” quote tweet.

#### Dev.to/Medium

- Optimize titles and headings for SEO (e.g., “RAG”, “Next.js 14”, “OpenAI”, “Pinecone”).
- Add alt text to all images.
- Cross-post and link back to your portfolio case study.

#### Reddit

- Focus on learnings and trade-offs, not just self-promo.
- Be ready to answer technical questions and accept constructive criticism.
